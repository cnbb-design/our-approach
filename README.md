# Our Approach to Design

**Cowboys & Beans** is a small independent publishing and arts collective. This repository is our design research and system documentation — released into the public domain, because we believe design knowledge should be common property.

William Morris didn't design wallpapers; he designed *systems for generating patterns*. In that spirit, this isn't a style guide that tells you what colour to use on a button. It's a collection of principles, research, and practical systems for building visual work that is grounded in perceptual science, mathematical proportion, and the conviction that good design is engineering.

## Mission chairs, not metal folding chairs

The [Arts & Crafts movement](philosophy/layout/#part-iv) understood that beauty and utility are not at odds — that a well-made chair serves you better *and* dignifies the room. Our design system works the same way. It doesn't produce "a layout"; it produces an infinite family of layouts, all consistent, all fluid, all grounded in the same mathematical structure, and every one of them different.
